TRAINING




1. Click on the "Complete training" button to continue to the Game Menu. You can then see a list of paths.

2. Select the path of your choice to begin the game.

3. You will be asked a series of questions . Answer them correctly to complete and conquer that path.

4. On completing any path you will be provided with a choice to select from the remaining paths.

5. You are provided with a restart button on every page, so if you feel like restarting the game, go for it.
